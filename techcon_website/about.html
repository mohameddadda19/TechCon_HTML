Our History

TechCon began in 2010 as a small meetup of developers and has since grown into one of the most influential tech conferences in the world. Over the years, it has hosted thousands of attendees, countless talks, and groundbreaking technology showcases.

Our Mission

TechCon is dedicated to advancing innovation, connecting tech leaders and creators, and fostering an inclusive environment where new ideas thrive. Our mission is to empower the next generation of tech innovators through learning and collaboration.
